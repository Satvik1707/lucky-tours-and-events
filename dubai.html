Your Journey Starts Here

Dubai, one of the seven united Arab emirates of the eponymous small country, really leaves an impression with all its splendour and unique buildings. Travelling to Dubai is travelling to the city of contrasts where the vibrancy of a modern metropolis and the traditional desert life are intertwined. Here you can experience world class luxury resorts and extreme adventures. It is the most popular and attractive destination that every visitor wants to experience.

BURJ KHALIFA:- Marvel at Dubai’s landmarks from high up. An unforgettable view awaits Burj Khalifa, up on the 125th floor of the world’s tallest building. The world’s fastest lift will take you to all the way up to the viewing platform. The top gives a view of the Dubai Fountain show, which moves in sync with the music that’s being played at the viewing platform. You can get a better experience of the tallest freestanding structure in the world.

MUSEUM OF THE FUTURE:- It is an exhibition space for innovative and futuristic ideologies, services, and products ,Located in the Financial District of Dubai. Explore what’s gonna happen in future by visiting all attractions.

DUBAI AQUARIUM AND UNDERWATER ZOO:- Dubai Aquarium & Underwater Zoo, offers fascinating insight into the 10-million-liter Dubai Aquarium tank, located on the Ground Level of The Dubai Mall, is one of the largest suspended aquariums in the world. It houses thousands of aquatic animals, comprising over 140 species,300 Sharks and Rays live in this tank, including one of the largest collections of Sand Tiger Sharks in the world. marine life by showcasing 300+ species of marine animals, comprising over 140 species. Over 400 Sharks and Rays live in our 10 million litre tank, including Sand Tiger Sharks, Giant Groupers, and a host of other marine species.

DUBAI MIRACLE GARDEN:- Miracle Garden Dubai is a horticultural attraction aimed at both locals and tourists and is also known as the world's largest natural flower garden. More than 150 million flowers and 120 plant varieties are exotically arranged, in different structures and shapes, to form colorful arches and patterns.

MALL OF THE EMIRATES:- The multi-level shopping mall currently features more than 630 retail outlets, 7900 parking spaces, over 100 restaurants and cafes, 80 luxury stores and 250 flagship stores. Which attracts the tourists .shops in Mall of emirates range from luxury brands and lifestyle outlets to popular street fashion stores.
